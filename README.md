# Retail-sales-performance-dashboard
An interactive dashboard designed to analyze retail sales data, track key performance metrics, and uncover trends in revenue, customer behavior, and product performance.
📊 Retail-Sales-Performance-Dashboard (Interactive Dashboard)
🎯 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze retail sales data to track business performance, identify key trends, and support data-driven decision-making. This dashboard helps stakeholders understand sales patterns, customer behavior, and product performance to improve overall business growth.


❓ Questions (KPIs)
What is the total sales, profit, and number of orders?
Which month has the highest sales and profit?
Which region contributes the most to sales?
What are the top-selling products and categories?
Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
What is the sales and profit trend over time?
Which products are generating losses?
Contribution of each category to total sales
⚙️ Process
Verified dataset for missing values and handled inconsistencies
Cleaned and transformed data (data types, formatting, null values)
Created calculated fields (Total Sales, Profit, Profit Margin)
Built pivot tables / visualizations based on KPIs
Designed an interactive dashboard using slicers/filters (Region, Category, Time)
Combined all visuals into one dynamic dashboard

📈 Project Insight
Sales show a clear seasonal trend with peak performance in certain months
A few product categories contribute the majority of total revenue
Some products generate high sales but low or negative profit
Certain regions consistently outperform others in terms of revenue
Repeat customers contribute significantly to total sales
Discounts and pricing strategies directly impact profitability
✅ Final Conclusion

To improve retail sales performance, the business should focus on high-performing products and regions while addressing low-profit or loss-making items. Optimizing pricing and discount strategies can help increase profitability.

Additionally, targeting high-value customer segments and improving inventory management will enhance overall sales efficiency and business growth.
